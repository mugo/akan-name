My Akan name generator

This is an application that collects your birthdate information, calculates the weekday on which you were born, and then generates your Akan Name based on your gender. Akan names originate from Ghanaian culture. It is common practice in Ghana for children to receive a first name known as a 'day name,' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.

To discover your Akan name, simply complete the form below and click the 'Generate Name' button.
